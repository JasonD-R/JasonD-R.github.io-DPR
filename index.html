History

Purdue University - December 9th, 2004

Delta Pi Rho was founded on Decmeber 9th, 2004 at Purdue University in West Lafayette, Indiana by four proud latinos!

These four Gentlemen had a new vision to empower the Latino community. on Four Pillars: Academics, Loyalty, Leadership, and Hermanos Unidos. The founders are Michael Carrillo, Luis Moreno, Ricardo Ramos, and Richard Rosales.

Javier "Turbo" Meraz
